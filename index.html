Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services and logistics capability.

What does Genex Logistics specialize in?

Genex Logistics is a premier 3PL supply chain and warehousing logistics company. We specialize in high-complexity logistics sectors, including Just-In-Time production supply, express road distribution, hazmat chemical storage, cold-chain pharmaceutical setups, and complex project freight forwarding.

Do you operate nationwide?

Yes, Genex operates an extensive PAN India network across 28 states and Union Territories. We manage a robust infrastructure of over 5 million square feet of warehousing space and maintain a comprehensive cargo shipping network.

What is your commitment to sustainability?

Sustainability is core to Genex. We optimize transport routes to decrease carbon emissions, utilize energy-efficient lighting inside our warehousing facilities, manage eco-friendly kitting and waste recycling, and are working towards carbon-neutral operations.

How do you support complex B2B supply chains?

We offer integrated systems linking raw material sourcing to production lines (production line feeding) and down to client shipping hubs. With precise inventory tracking, bonded logistics, and specialized handling, we keep operations running with 99.9% uptime.
