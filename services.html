What is Free Trade Warehousing Zone (FTWZ)?

FTWZs are specialized SEZs designed specifically for warehousing, trading, and logistics services. Importing through a Genex FTWZ allows global businesses to defer customs duty payments, perform repackaging, label goods, and optimize tax cashflows before delivering to local buyers.

What is Milk Run Logistics?

Milk Run logistics is a shipping routing method where a single vehicle visits multiple local suppliers to collect partial cargo loads before delivering to a centralized manufacturer, or vice versa. This maximizes vehicle utilization and keeps your materials replenishment schedules fully synchronized.
