Company Overview

Built on Thought Leadership, Sustainability, and Operational Integrity.

Uptime-Oriented Operations

Genex Logistics is India's leading 3PL provider specializing in high-complexity logistics sectors. While generic warehouse providers sell empty square footage, we specialize in high-uptime logistics models where a minor supply bottleneck can halt production operations or disappoint enterprise clients.

Founded with a core focus on client customization, process integration, and absolute compliance, we operate a nationwide network supporting over 12 industries. Our systems handle everything from material intake, kitting, line feeding, and distribution to secure shipping and global freight management.

Sustainability is integrated across every layer. From transport route optimizations that curtail emissions to LED systems inside warehousing grids, we support green logistics practices daily.

Our Journey

Tracing our path from a local logistics specialist to a PAN India 3PL industry leader.

2011

Inception

Genex Logistics is established in New Delhi, securing its first major automotive parts warehousing and kitting contract in the NCR.

2014

PAN India Expansion

Expands transport fleets and opens regional distribution hubs across Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, and Karnataka, providing end-to-end multi-state cargo routes.

2018

Smart Technology Integration

Integrates advanced Warehouse Management Systems (WMS) and deploys multi-client shared warehousing structures to support e-commerce client growth.
